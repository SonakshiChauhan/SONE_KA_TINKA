What's The Problem

  • According to the WHO, in 2012 ambient air pollution contributed to 6.7 % of all deaths worldwide. In particular, 16% of lung cancer deaths,11% of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease deaths , and more than 20% of ischaemic heart disease and stroke are associated with ambient fine particulate matter. The economic cost of the approximate 600 000 premature deaths and of the diseases caused by air pollution in the WHO European Region in 2010 has been estimated in Euro 1.5 trillion.People in Delhi/NCR are having their annual tryst with the deadly winter air pollution where toxic clouds of smog engulf this region every year around the winter months. Contributing to nearly 30% of winter air pollution in Delhi, stubble burning across the nearby states of Punjab, Haryana is attributed as one of the major factors for the existence of Air Pollution in the region during this period.

  • During winters we get to see that around 30% contribution of air pollution because of stubble burning which spread as a smog which is responsible for various disease.

  • Now the point is that why farmers compelled to burn these stubble basically these stubble when start decomposing in it release excessive methane gas which affect human and animal , dumping of these stubble is not pocket friendly for farmers thats why they rely on the option of stubble burning which cause pollution.

  • Now point is that What is the solution of stubble burning how farmers overcome with the problem which we discuss about We are introducing a project "sone ka tinka" where we can talk about how we convert these stubble as a source of income.

Market Trends

  • In past few years demand of online food delivery and streets food increase drastically which requires disposable cups ,plates, straw, and etc currently in market only plastic disposable cups , due to high demands of disposable supply is very limited it is harder full-fill the demand of the market.So we are introducing an idea manufacturing disposable cups , plates and others stuff from stubble.

  • The disposable manufactured from stubble is completely environment friendly and bio-degradable in nature. Like other paper cups and plates these stubble cups have good holding capacity of 5 hours. All we have to do is to just convert stubble into the cellulose and further modify into desire shape.The disposable cups have comparatively low cost than traditional cups

  • Our main focus is make environment friendly product and cost efficient which also empower farmers as a source of income and the stubble which causes air pollution now acting as environment saver by replacing traditional plastic disposable cups.

HOW TO DO IT

STEP 1: First of all collecting raw materials ie. Stubble from farmers and transport to warehouse.

STEP 2: Separate between stubble and small stones which is stage one filtration and breakdown into small pices.

STEP 3: Start washing of small pices which is second stage of filtration and add softening reagent.

STEP 4: Start washing of small pices which is second stage of filtration and add softening reagent.

STEP 5: With the help of molding machine convert cellulose into desire shape ie. cups , plates ,straws, etc.

The important part of this whole procedure is adhesive of the cellulose initially we are concern about adhesivety factor but it is upto the mark in case of cellulose of stubble.
